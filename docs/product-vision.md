# Product Vision

## Cabinet Shop Operating System

Cabinet Shop Platform is a cabinet shop operating system: a single place to manage the flow of custom cabinet work from lead to closeout.

The product should help a shop answer the questions that matter every day:

- What jobs are active?
- Where is each job in the shop?
- Who owns the next step?
- What files, drawings, approvals, and notes are current?
- What is blocked?
- What needs to happen today?

## Niche Vs. General Construction Tools

General construction tools are usually designed around broad project management, field coordination, RFIs, change orders, and jobsite documentation. Cabinet shops need some of that, but their daily reality is more specific:

- Design revisions and customer approvals
- Materials and hardware purchasing
- Cut lists, milling, face frames, assembly, sanding, finishing, and final assembly
- Shop scheduling and department handoffs
- Delivery and install coordination
- Quality control before work leaves the shop

This product should win by being narrower, clearer, and more useful to cabinet shops than a general-purpose system.

## Built By Cabinet Guys, For Cabinet Guys

The product voice should feel practical, shop-aware, and grounded. It should avoid buzzwords and instead reflect the real language of cabinet work: drawings, parts, finish, install, punch, hardware, delivery, approvals, and rework.

## Target Users

- Cabinet shop owners
- Operations managers
- Sales teams
- Designers
- Purchasers
- Shop leads
- Department workers
- Finish teams
- Installers
- Office coordinators

## Why This Matters

Cabinet shops lose time and money when job information is scattered. A purpose-built system can reduce rework, improve schedule confidence, clarify responsibility, and make the whole shop easier to manage.
